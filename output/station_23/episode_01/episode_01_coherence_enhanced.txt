=== SCENE 1: INT. TOM'S APARTMENT - EARLY MORNING ===
[SFX: ALARM CLOCK BEEPING, then CLICKS OFF. FADE IN GENTLE AMBIENT CITY SOUNDS DISTANT THROUGH WINDOW. FAINT COFFEE MAKER STARTING UP.]

[SFX: LIGHT SCRATCHING OF PEN ON PAPER.]

TOM
(into phone voice recorder)
Day 147 of The Daily Lift. Good morning, champions. Today's thought: Sometimes the smallest step in the right direction ends up being the biggest step of your life. Tip toe if you must, but take the step. Remember, you are stronger than you think. Make today count.

[SFX: PEN SETS DOWN. KEYBOARD TYPING RHYTHMICALLY.]

TOM
(muttering to himself)
Alright, let's send this to my list... select all... send... and... good to go.

[SFX: MOUSE CLICK. COMPUTER CONFIRMATION CHIME.]

TOM
(sighs contentedly)
One more day, one more life potentially changed.

[SFX: RUSTLING OF CLOTHES. COFFEE POURING INTO MUG.]

TOM
Now for the important stuff—caffeine.

[SFX: FADE TO BLACK]

=== SCENE 2: INT. JULIA'S BEDROOM - EARLY MORNING ===
[SFX: PHONE BUZZING VIBRATES ON NIGHTSTAND. JULIA GROANS. RAIN GENTLY TAPPING AGAINST WINDOW.]

[SFX: PHONE BUZZES AGAIN. JULIA SHUFFLES IN BED. HAND SLAPS NIGHTSTAND SEARCHING FOR PHONE.]

JULIA
(groggy, to herself)
Five more minutes...

[SFX: PHONE BUZZES A THIRD TIME. LOUDER. JULIA JERKS UPRIGHT. SHE GRABS PHONE. TEXT MESSAGE ALERT CHIMES.]

[SFX: PHONE VOICE READING TEXT.]

PHONE VOICE
(automated, filtered)
Day 147 of The Daily Lift. Good morning, champions. Today's thought: Sometimes the smallest step in the right direction ends up being the biggest step of your life...

JULIA
(confused, annoyed)
What the—? Who is this?

[SFX: FINGERS RAPIDLY TAPPING PHONE SCREEN.]

JULIA
Wrong number. Must be spam. I don't recognize this contact at all.

[SFX: PHONE THUMPS ON MATTRESS. JULIA SWINGS LEGS OUT OF BED, FEET PADDING ON FLOOR.]

JULIA
(to herself, thoughtful)
Smallest step in the right direction... right. If only it were that simple.

[SFX: FAINT SOUND OF RAIN MIXES WITH ALARM CLOCK BEEPING. FADE TO BLACK.]

=== SCENE 3: INT. HOSPITAL HALLWAY - MORNING ===
[SFX: HOSPITAL HALLWAY AMBIENCE—DISTANT BEEPING MACHINES, PAGERS, SOFT CONVERSATIONS. FOOTSTEPS SQUEAK ON LINOLEUM.]

DR. MARTINEZ
(filtered, approaching)
Dr. Evans! Julia! Got a moment?

JULIA
(distracted)
Dr. Martinez. Of course. What's up?

[SFX: FOOTSTEPS STOP. MEDICAL CART PASSES BY. DR. MARTINEZ'S VOICE IS CLEARER NOW.]

DR. MARTINEZ
The Miller case. Family wants to speak with someone about transition options. I thought with your background in palliative care...

JULIA
(abruptly, voice tight)
I can't.

DR. MARTINEZ
(surprised)
Is everything alright?

JULIA
(quickly, avoiding eye contact)
I'm just—swamped with paperwork. Ask Linda. She's better with that sort of thing.

[SFX: BRIEF PAUSE. PAGER BEEPS IN DISTANCE. NURSE LINDA APPROACHES. CART WHEELS SQUEAKING.]

NURSE LINDA
Did someone say my name?

DR. MARTINEZ
Julia thinks you'd be better with the Miller family. About transition options.

NURSE LINDA
(concerned)
Julia, you usually handle these so well.

JULIA
(evasive, voice tightening)
I have that report due, remember? The one that's already late?

[SFX: RAPID FOOTSTEPS WALKING AWAY. DOOR SWINGS SHARPLY SHUT.]

NURSE LINDA
(concerned, to Dr. Martinez)
She's been like this all week. Ever since her mother...

DR. MARTINEZ
(gently)
I know. Give her space. She'll come around when she's ready.

[SFX: BOTH WALK AWAY, FOOTSTEPS FADING. INTERCOM PAGES DOCTOR. FADE TO BLACK.]

=== SCENE 4: INT. TOM'S OFFICE - AFTERNOON ===
[SFX: MODERN OFFICE AMBIENCE—DISTANT KEYBOARD TYPING, AIR CONDITIONING HUM.]

[SFX: KNOCK ON DOOR. DOOR OPENS.]

MARK
(excited)
Tom! Got a minute?

TOM
(chuckles)
For you, Mark, always. Come in.

[SFX: DOOR CLOSES. FOOTSTEPS APPROACH.]

MARK
Just heard from Sarah—the one who was struggling with the career change?

TOM
(optimistic)
And?

MARK
She took the leap! Quit her job, enrolled in that design program. Said your coaching gave her the courage.

TOM
(warmly)
That's what we do—help people find their path.

MARK
You're growing the business fast. We've already hit our quarterly target.

TOM
It's not about targets, Mark. It's about lives changed.

[SFX: KEYBOARD TAPPING.]

MARK
Right, but... you need to think about scaling. Maybe bring on another coach?

TOM
(meditatively)
Maybe. Right now, I like the personal touch. Each message... each call... matters.

[SFX: PHONE VIBRATES. TOM PICKS IT UP.]

TOM
Sorry, need to take this.

[SFX: FADE TO BLACK.]

=== SCENE 5: INT. JULIA'S OFFICE - EVENING ===
[SFX: QUIET OFFICE AMBIENCE—CLOCK TICKING RHYTHMICALLY, KEYBOARD CLACKING SLOWLY.]

[SFX: JULIA SITS HEAVILY IN CHAIR. CHAIR SQUEAKS UNDER WEIGHT. DEEP SIGH.]

JULIA
(to herself)
Another day.

[SFX: MOUSE CLICK. KEYBOARD TYPING RAPIDLY THEN SLOWLY. STOPS.]

JULIA
(muttering)
Transition options... end-of-life care... how can I help others when I can't even—

[SFX: PHONE BUZZES INSISTENTLY. SHE STARTLES, THEN PICKS IT UP. TEXT MESSAGE ALERT CHIMES.]

[SFX: FINGERS TAPPING PHONE SCREEN. DEEP BREATH.]

JULIA
(reading quietly, then with emphasis)
"Sometimes the smallest step in the right direction ends up being the biggest step of your life..."

[SFX: BRIEF PAUSE. CLOCK TICKS LOUDLY.]

JULIA
(whispering)
Right direction...

[SFX: PHONE SETS DOWN ON DESK WITH SOFT THUD. PAUSE. DRAWER OPENS WITH SQUEAK. PAPERS RUSTLE.]

[SFX: JULIA PULLS OUT A FRAMED PHOTO. FINGERS TRACING FRAME. GENTLY SETS IT ON DESK.]

JULIA
(to the photo, voice breaking slightly)
I miss you, Mom. Every day gets a little harder, not easier.

[SFX: JULIA CLEARS THROAT. TEARDROP HITS DESK. WIND HOWLS OUTSIDE WINDOW. FADE TO BLACK.]

=== SCENE 6: INT. TOM'S APARTMENT - NIGHT ===
[SFX: QUIET APARTMENT AMBIENCE—REFRIGERATOR HUMMING, DISTANT SIREN.]

[SFX: TOM ENTERS. KEYS ON COUNTER. BAG SETS DOWN.]

TOM
(to himself)
Long day.

[SFX: COFFEE MACHINE CLICKS ON. MUG CERAMIC CLINKS.]

TOM
Let's see... emails, then tomorrow's message.

[SFX: LAPTOP OPENS. KEYBOARD TAPPING.]

[SFX: NOTIFICATION DINGS. TOM HESITATES.]

TOM
(confused)
Unknown number?

[SFX: KEYBOARD TAPS. MESSAGE OPENS.]

TOM
(reading aloud)
"Whoever you are, I don't know you, but your message came at the right time. Thank you."

[SFX: LONG PAUSE. MOUSE CLICKS.]

TOM
(to himself)
Wrong number... but right message?

[SFX: BRIEF PAUSE. TOM SMILES THOUGHTFULLY. MOUSE CLICKS AGAIN. TYPING SLOWLY.]

TOM
(typing as he speaks)
"Glad it helped. Sometimes the universe works in mysterious ways. Tom."

[SFX: MESSAGE SENDS. LAPTOP CLOSES. COFFEE SIP.]

TOM
(thoughtfully)
Mysterious ways indeed.

[SFX: FADE TO BLACK.]
